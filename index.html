Introduction

Every animal deserves a life filled with love, safety, and purpose. At Pawsitive Future, we rescue dogs and cats who have been abandoned, neglected, or surrendered, and give them the medical care and emotional support they need to heal. But our mission doesn’t end there—we also match each pet with a compassionate adopter who will provide a lifelong home. By choosing to adopt, you become part of a community that believes in second chances and creates brighter futures for animals and people alike.

About Us

Turning Second Chances into Forever Homes

Pawsitive Future is a volunteer-powered rescue based in Ottawa. Since 2020 we’ve saved more than 600 animals from overcrowded shelters and medical emergencies. Our team blends veterinary expertise with community outreach to ensure every pet receives the care, training, and love they deserve—before and after adoption.
