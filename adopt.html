Adoption Journey: 4 Simple Steps

Hi, I’m Maya, your friendly guide through the process.
First, I’ll help you complete the quick online application so we understand your lifestyle. Next, we’ll arrange a meet-and-greet at the shelter to see if sparks fly.
If all goes well, you’ll enjoy a seven-day home trial where I’m just a call away for questions. Finally, we’ll sign the paperwork together and snap that happy “Gotcha Day” photo as your new best friend trots into their forever home.

Step 1 — Submit Application

Fill out our online form so we can learn about your lifestyle and adoption goals.

Step 2 — Meet & Greet

Meet the dog with an adoption counselor to confirm the chemistry is right.

Step 3 — Home Trial

Enjoy a 7–14-day trial stay to make sure everyone adjusts comfortably.

Step 4 — Make It Official

Sign the adoption contract and welcome your new family member home!
